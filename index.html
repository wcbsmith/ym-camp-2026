Where We'll Be

Three Epic Spots

From a mountain cabin with its own creek to a slot canyon carved through conglomerate rock — this trip covers serious ground.

Our Base Camp

Skyline Mountain Resort

Fairview, Utah ~6,100 ft elevation 1 hr 20 min from SLC

A family cabin with a creek running through the property, mountain views in every direction, and a sky dark enough to actually see the Milky Way. Some guys sleep inside, some pitch tents outside — both are part of the experience.

Full kitchen Fire pit Tent camping Creek access Stargazing
Thursday's Big Day

Palisade State Park

Sterling, Utah ~35 min from cabin

A lake in the middle of Utah's high plateau country — sand beach, warm water in July, and boats to rent. The perfect counterbalance to canyon scrambling. Spend six hours in the water and don't look back.

Swimming Sand beach Paddleboards Kayaking Fishing Lawn games
Friday's Adventure

Maple Canyon

Sanpete County, Utah ~45 min from cabin

One of Utah's most unique geological spots — conglomerate rock walls packed with stones like a giant's mosaic. Over 550 climbing routes, a famous narrow slot canyon called Box Canyon, and a trail leading to a natural arch. The canyon walls shade everything, so it stays cool even in July.

Bouldering Box Canyon scramble Arch Trail Rock climbing Exploring Shaded trails
